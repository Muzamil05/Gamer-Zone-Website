    DreamcastGuy

    Game: Sonic Adventure

    Console: Sega Dreamcast

    Most modern Sonic games have mostly been a miss, Sonic adventure is a fantastic game. With the franchises first attempt into 3D they nailed this game. Being able to play as different characters is awesome and all of the levels are really great. I'm glad that now with the release of Sonic Frontiers, Sega has finally figured out what to do with modern Sonic, however this game will always be the best Sonic game for me that has been released.
