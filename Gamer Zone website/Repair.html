    At Gamer Zone we repair your Retro Consoles! we currently repair Consoles from the NES all the way to the PS3 from 2006. Any Older or newer Consoles we unfortunately do not repair. We also repair handheld consoles such as the Gameboy Consoles. Please note that the repairs we do depend on if we have the parts available to repair the console. In addition, we do not repair console models that have not been released in the UK. These are the following Consoles that we can repair and their prices for repairing. Prices for these may vary. If you would like to send a console for us to repair, then please click the button below that will take you to a repair form.
